The Problem

Managing and organizing your automation stack is harder than ever. Between disconnected tools and poor integration, teams waste hours context-switching instead of building.

  • Too many disconnected tools leading to chaos.
  • Wasted time managing tasks instead of completing them.
  • Steep learning curves and brittle setups.

Our Solution

NodeBox unifies Python-powered nodes and local AI (Ollama) into a single canvas—so you can design, run, and manage complex automations visually, without costly APIs.

  • Unified canvas to cut down context switching.
  • Fast, modern UI designed for focus and clarity.
  • Local-first AI with Ollama—own your workflows.
